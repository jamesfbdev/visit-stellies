Top three activities to do in Stellenbosch!

Wine tasting

With some of the top wineries in the world, Stellenbosch is one of the best places to enjoy wine in picturesque settings

Heritage Tours

Stellenbosch has some incredible heritage. Boasting one of the best universities in South Africa, it's history goes back to 1648

Sculpture Gardens

Stellenbosch is not only known for wine, the art scene here is incredible. Spend some time at the famous Dylan Lewis Sculpture Garden
